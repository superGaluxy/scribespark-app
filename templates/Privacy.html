Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 13, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to ScribeSpark ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website, scribespark.online (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Our tools are designed to be used without requiring you to create an account or provide any personal information. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data like your name, email address, or IP address.

The text, topics, and images you input into our AI tools are sent to our third-party AI provider (Google's Gemini API) for processing. This data is used solely to generate a response for you and is not stored or logged by us. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy for more information on how they handle data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: To power our AI tools. Your inputs are sent to Google for processing.
  • Adsterra: We may use Adsterra to display advertisements on our website. Adsterra may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Adsterra's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare: For website performance and security. Cloudflare may collect standard web traffic data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Us page.
